# Gmail Label Organization Strategy

## Label Categories for Email Management

| Label Category | Labels |
| --- | --- |
| To Respond | Emails that need your reply, Awaiting Reply |
| FYI | Informational emails |
| Meeting Update | Meeting Update, Calendar notifications |
| Actioned | Actioned, Completed email threads |
| Cold Email | Cold Email, Unsolicited emails |
| Notification | Notification, Service notifications |
| Marketing | Marketing, Promotional emails |

## Usage Notes
- **To Respond**: Clear call-to-action emails requiring a reply
- **FYI**: Informational content for reference, no action needed
- **Meeting Update**: Calendar invites, meeting notes, scheduling
- **Actioned**: Completed tasks, resolved issues, finished discussions
- **Cold Email**: Unwanted/solicited marketing or sales emails
- **Notification**: System alerts, service notifications, automated messages
- **Marketing**: Promotional content, newsletters, advertising
