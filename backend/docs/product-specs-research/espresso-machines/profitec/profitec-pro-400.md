# Profitec Pro 400 Espresso Machine - Technical Specifications

## Overview
The Profitec Pro 400 is a compact heat exchanger espresso machine that offers simultaneous brewing and steaming capabilities with professional-grade features in a space-saving design.

## Boiler System
- **Type**: Heat exchanger (HX) system
- **Material**: Stainless steel boiler with insulation
- **Capacity**: 1.6 liters
- **Heating Element**: Stainless steel
- **Temperature Control**: 3 PID-controlled boiler temperatures:
  - 248°F (120°C)
  - 255.2°F (124°C)
  - 262.4°F (128°C)

## Power Specifications
- **EU/UK/CH/AU**: 230V, 1400W
- **US**: 115V, 1500W

## Pump System
- **Type**: Vibration pump (Ulka EX5)
- **Features**: 
  - Adjustable expansion valve
  - Dual gauge for boiler and pump pressure

## Dimensions & Weight
- **Width**: 228 mm (8.98")
- **Depth**: 
  - 448 mm (17.64") without portafilter
  - 558 mm (21.97") with portafilter
- **Height**: 372 mm (14.65")
- **Weight**: 21.5 kg (47.4 lbs)

## Water System
- **Water Tank Capacity**: 2.8 liters
- **Water Tank**: Removable with built-in adapter
- **Safety**: Automatic low water volume shut-off

## Key Features
- **Group Head**: E61 brew group with stainless steel mushroom
- **PID Control**: 3 selectable temperature settings
- **Pre-infusion**: ON/OFF capability
- **ECO Mode**: ON/OFF for energy savings
- **Pressure Gauges**: Dual gauge showing both boiler and pump pressure
- **Portafilter**: 58mm commercial size

## Control Features
- PID display with temperature selection
- Pre-infusion control
- ECO mode programming
- Temperature selection between 3 preset levels

## Build Quality
- Commercial-grade components
- Stainless steel boiler
- E61 group head for temperature stability
- Professional build quality throughout

## Advantages Over Single Boiler
- Simultaneous brewing and steaming
- No temperature surfing required
- Consistent brewing temperature
- Faster workflow for milk-based drinks

## Notes
- Compact footprint for a heat exchanger machine
- Professional features in a home-friendly size
- E61 group head provides excellent temperature stability
- Heat exchanger design ideal for milk-based drink enthusiasts
