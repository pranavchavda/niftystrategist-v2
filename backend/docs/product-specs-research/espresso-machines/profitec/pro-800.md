# Profitec Pro 800 Lever Specifications Research

## Product Information
- **SKU**: Not specified in research
- **Product Name**: Profitec Pro 800 Lever Espresso Machine
- **Handle**: profitec-pro-800-espresso-machine
- **Status**: Active (2022 updated version available)

## Confirmed Specifications

### Dimensions & Weight
- **Height**: 28.8 inches (740mm with lever up)
- **Width**: 13.4 inches (339mm)
- **Depth**: 22.9 inches (505mm without portafilter, 593mm with)
- **Weight**: 78 lbs

### Power & Electrical
- **Max Wattage**: 1500W
- **Voltage**: 110/120V
- **Plug Type**: Standard 15 amp
- **Cord Length**: 5 feet

### Boiler System
- **Type**: Single boiler (steam/brew)
- **Boiler Material**: Copper
- **Boiler Capacity**: 3.5L (118.35 oz)
- **Heating Element**: 1500W

### Temperature Control
- **PID Controller**: Yes
- **PID Location**: Hidden behind water collection tray
- **Temperature Adjustment**: Digital, individual degree adjustment

### Brew Group
- **Type**: Spring lever (hand lever)
- **Weight**: 17 lbs (7.8kg) heavy-duty lever group
- **Portafilter Size**: 58mm (commercial grade)
- **Pre-infusion**: True low-pressure (1-1.5 bar from boiler)
- **Brew Pressure**: Spring-loaded piston delivery

### Pump System
- **Type**: Vibratory (not used for brewing - lever provides pressure)
- **Purpose**: Water supply to boiler only

### Water System
- **Reservoir Capacity**: 3L (101 oz)
- **Reservoir Material**: BPA-free plastic
- **Removable**: Yes
- **Low Water Shutoff**: Yes
- **Plumbable**: Yes (direct plumb or reservoir)

### Additional Features
- **Pressure Gauges**: 2 (pump pressure and steam boiler pressure)
- **Steam Wand**: Stainless steel, articulating, full rotation
- **Hot Water Dispenser**: Yes
- **Lever Valves**: Joystick-style for steam and hot water
- **Wood Accents**: American walnut (handles, lever, portafilter)
- **Gauge**: Large pine-green pressure gauge (2022 update)
- **Frame**: Stainless steel construction
- **Housing Material**: Stainless steel
- **Drip Tray Capacity**: 1L (33.81 oz)
- **Warm-up Time**: 30 minutes

### Included Accessories
- Single spout portafilter
- Double spout portafilter
- Bottomless portafilter
- 1 cup filter basket (7g)
- 2 cup filter basket (14g)
- 21g filter basket
- Tamper
- Cleaning brush

### Lever Operation
- **Pre-infusion**: Pull lever down to start, hot water flows and cools to brew temp
- **Extraction**: Lever returns to position, spring applies pressure
- **Pressure Profile**: Manual control via lever manipulation
