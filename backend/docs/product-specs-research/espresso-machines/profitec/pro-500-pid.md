# Profitec Pro 500 PID Specifications Research

## Product Information
- **SKU**: Not specified in research
- **Product Name**: Profitec Pro 500 PID Espresso Machine
- **Handle**: profitec-pro-500-pid-espresso-machine
- **Status**: Active (available at multiple retailers)

## Confirmed Specifications

### Dimensions & Weight
- **Height**: 15.5 inches (39.3 cm)
- **Width**: 11.9 inches (30.2 cm)
- **Depth**: 17.6 inches (44.7 cm) / 55.5 cm with portafilter
- **Weight**: 51 lbs (23.1 kg) [Clive] / 50 lbs [other sources]

### Power & Electrical
- **Power**: 1400W
- **Voltage**: 110/120V
- **Plug Type**: Standard 15 amp
- **Cord Length**: Not specified

### Boiler System
- **Type**: Heat Exchanger
- **Boiler Material**: Stainless steel
- **Boiler Capacity**: 2L (67.6 oz)
- **Insulated**: Yes (velcro wrap)
- **Heating Element**: 1400W
- **Steam Pressure**: 1.8 BAR (best in class)
- **Auto-fill**: Yes

### Temperature Control
- **PID Controller**: Yes
- **PID Location**: Hidden under drip tray (pre-2022) / Face of machine (2022 update)
- **Shot Timer**: Yes (2022 update - PID doubles as shot timer)
- **Temperature Range**: Not specified
- **Eco Mode**: Programmable (2022 update)

### Group Head
- **Type**: E61
- **Activation**: Lever activated
- **Portafilter Size**: 58mm
- **Pre-infusion**: Standard E61 thermosyphon

### Pump System
- **Type**: Vibratory (Ulka EX5)
- **Noise Level**: Notably quiet
- **Brew Pressure**: 9 bar (recommended)
- **Adjustable Pressure**: Yes

### Water System
- **Reservoir Capacity**: 3L (100 oz)
- **Reservoir Material**: BPA-free plastic
- **Removable**: Yes
- **Low Water Shutoff**: Yes
- **Plumbable**: No

### Additional Features
- **Pressure Gauges**: 2 (boiler pressure left, brew pressure right)
- **Gauge Color**: Black (2022 update)
- **Steam Wand**: Double-wall, cool-touch
- **Hot Water Dispenser**: Double-wall, cool-touch
- **Cup Warmer**: Passive
- **Warm-up Time**: 15-20 minutes
- **Housing Material**: Polished stainless steel
- **Frame**: Single piece internal steel frame

### Included Accessories
- 2x 58mm portafilters
- Tamper
- Single and double shot baskets (non-pressurized)
- Blind basket
- Cleaning brush
- Cleaning cloth
- User manual

- PID eliminates need for cooling flush typical of HX machines
- German engineering with focus on maintenance accessibility
