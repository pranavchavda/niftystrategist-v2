## Confirmed Specifications

### Dimensions & Weight
- **Height**: 15.63 inches
- **Width**: 12 inches (13.5 inches including arms)
- **Depth**: 17.63 inches (22 inches including portafilter)
- **Weight**: 53 lbs

### Power & Electrical
- **Max Wattage**: 1400W
- **Voltage**: 110/120V
- **Plug Type**: Standard 15 amp
- **Cord Length**: 5 feet

### Boiler System
- **Type**: Dual Boiler
- **Brew Boiler**: 
  - Capacity: 0.75L
  - Power: 1000W
  - Material: Stainless steel
  - Temperature: 200°F (typical)
- **Steam Boiler**: 
  - Capacity: 1L
  - Power: 1400W
  - Material: Stainless steel
  - Temperature: 270°F (typical)
  - Pressure: Up to 2 bar
- **Insulated**: Yes
- **Auto-fill**: Yes

### Temperature Control
- **PID Controller**: Yes (dual PID - controls both boilers independently)
- **PID Display**: Front-facing
- **Shot Timer**: Yes (PID doubles as shot timer)
- **Temperature Adjustment**: Individual boiler control
- **Single Boiler Mode**: Steam boiler can be switched off

### Group Head
- **Type**: E61
- **Activation**: Lever activated
- **Portafilter Size**: 58mm
- **Pre-infusion**: E61 thermosyphon system

### Pump System
- **Type**: Vibratory
- **Noise Level**: One of the quietest vibratory pumps
- **Brew Pressure**: 9 bar (recommended)
- **Adjustable Pressure**: Yes (behind drip tray)

### Water System
- **Reservoir Capacity**: 3L (101 oz)
- **Reservoir Material**: BPA-free plastic
- **Removable**: Yes
- **Low Water Shutoff**: Yes
- **Plumbable**: No (reservoir only)

### Additional Features
- **Quick Steam Valves**: Joystick-style (Clive Coffee exclusive in North America)
- **Valve Offset**: 1" from center for enhanced workspace
- **Steam Wand**: Stainless steel, articulating, full rotation
- **Hot Water Dispenser**: Yes
- **Drip Tray Capacity**: 0.95L (32 oz)
- **Housing Material**: Stainless steel
- **Side Panels**: Matte black with cutouts
- **Warm-up Time**: 15 minutes (12 min to temp, 10 min Fast Heat mode for shots)

### Fast Heat-Up Feature
- **Function**: Overheats boiler for rapid warm-up
- **Time to Shots**: 10 minutes
- **Full Ready Time**: 11 minutes (with 20-second flush)

### Warranty
- **Coverage**: 3 years (Clive Coffee)
